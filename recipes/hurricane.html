Hurricane Cocktail

A Hurricane is a fruity, tropical inspired cocktail that is specific to the French Quarter of New Orleans.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz. light rum
  • 2 oz. dark rum
  • 2 oz. passion fruit juice
  • 1 oz. orange juice
  • Â½ oz. fresh lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon simple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon grenadine
  • Garnish: orange slice and cherry

    • Steps

    1. Shake all ingredients in a cocktail shaker with ice and strain into a hurricane glass filled with ice. Garnish with a cheery and an orange slice.
    2. Enjoy!
